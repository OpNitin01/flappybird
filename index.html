Tip: On mobile tap anywhere to flap. Hold to repeatedly flap. Use left/right edge taps to move horizontally.
How to play
  1. Tap / press Space / Up to flap upward.
  2. Navigate the bird through the gaps in the pipes.
  3. Score increases by passing pipes. Every 10 points speed increases slightly.
